FAQ

Common questions

Things business owners ask before getting started.

Is this different from SEO?
Yes. Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's blue-link results. GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers in tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. The signals are different — brand mentions, citability, structured data, and AI crawler access matter far more than backlinks for AI visibility. We do both, but GEO is where the growth is going.
How long before I see results?
Technical fixes like schema markup, robots.txt, and llms.txt can move your GEO score within two to four weeks. Brand authority and citability improvements take two to three months to compound. We track your score every month so you see exactly what is moving and what is not.
Do I need to change my whole website?
No. Most GEO fixes are additive — we add structured data, create an llms.txt file, update robots.txt, and rewrite specific content blocks. Your existing site stays intact. We work within your current CMS and only touch the pages and files that need updating.
I am already paying an SEO agency. Why do I need this?
Most SEO agencies are optimizing for a channel that is shrinking. AI-referred traffic grew 527% last year. Gartner predicts traditional search traffic drops 50% by 2028. Your SEO agency is working hard — on yesterday's problem. Citable handles the channel that is growing. Many of our clients run both in parallel.
What businesses is this for?
Any business where customers search for recommendations online. F&B, professional services, retail, property, healthcare, SaaS, e-commerce. If someone in your city might ask ChatGPT or Perplexity for a recommendation in your category, you need GEO. The audit will tell you exactly how visible you currently are.
What happens after the audit?
You receive two PDF reports — one covering your full marketing health, one covering your GEO visibility score. We walk through the findings on a 45-minute call. You will know exactly what is broken, what to fix first, and what the projected impact is. There is no pressure to continue. Most clients do.
